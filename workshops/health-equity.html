Health Equity Fundamentals

Building the Foundation for Equitable Care

Foundational concepts, root causes, and strategies to address health disparities.

Request This Workshop

Workshop Highlights

Understanding Core Health Equity Principles

Covers foundational concepts and the role of social determinants of health (SDOH) in patient outcomes.

Using Data for Health Equity

Teaches collection, analysis, and interpretation of data to identify disparities and measure equity initiative impacts.

Mitigating Bias and Promoting Cultural Competence

Develops skills to recognize and reduce unconscious biases while fostering inclusive, patient-centered practices.

Implementing Strategies for Equitable Practices

Presents actionable approaches including policy changes and community engagement initiatives.

Learning Outcomes

1

Define and Apply Health Equity Principles

Define and apply health equity principles to identify disparities in healthcare access and outcomes.

2

Analyze Social Determinants

Analyze social determinants and their impact on health outcomes across diverse populations.

3

Promote Culturally Sensitive Practices

Promote culturally sensitive practices that enhance patient care and community engagement.

4

Design Organizational Equity Strategies

Design and implement organizational equity strategies that address systemic barriers.

5

Leverage Data for Disparity Identification

Collect and analyze data to identify disparities and measure the effectiveness of equity initiatives.

Who Should Attend

  • All team members within healthcare organizations
  • Health equity professionals
  • Community leaders and organizers

Suggested Format

  • 90-minutes, Half-Day, or Full-Day
  • Onsite or Virtual
