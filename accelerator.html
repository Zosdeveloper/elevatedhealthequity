Health Equity Resilience Accelerator Program

Build Resilience. Navigate Change. Lead with Confidence.
A 4-week program for health equity practitioners navigating today's complex healthcare landscape.

What We'll Cover

Analyze Current Policy Impacts

Understand how recent executive orders, including restrictions on DEI programs, gender-affirming care, and civil rights enforcement.

Develop Legally Defensible Strategies

Create health equity initiatives that comply with anti-DEI policies while leveraging existing frameworks to protect care access for marginalized populations.

Strengthen Data Collection Under Constraints

Explore new approaches to collecting and utilizing data strategies that identify disparities while avoiding prohibited DEI terminology.

Build Coalition-Driven Solutions

Strengthen and mobilize with community partners to advance health equity efforts.
